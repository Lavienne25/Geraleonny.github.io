Manggrove Beach

The function of mangrove forests is to prevent beach erosion, beach erosion or abrasion
is a threat to the environment,Become a Soil Catalyst from Sea Water,marine environment,
Providing Broad Economic Impact,Animal Feed Sources, andMaintain Water and Air Quality.


Mangrove fruit can be processed into flour and various processed food ingredients such as
syrup, chips, dodol, and other processed snack foods

On this mangrove forest beach, we can catch crabs, plant mangrove trees, make chips from mangrove tree leaves,
play on the beach sand, swim on the beach, take a boat along the mangrove forest beach area,
take selfies in aesthetic places and we too can ride atv in the sand.
